FREEMANS

Reservation

POLICY

Freemans accepts reservations for parties of all sizes, 1 month in advance to the numerical date.

For parties of 13 or more, Freemans accepts bookings up to 1 year in advance.

Walk-ins for small parties are encouraged at any time.

A portion of the dining room is held for walk-in guests. We have two excellent bars for waiting and serve the full menu at both.

We seat only completed parties on a first come, first served basis.
